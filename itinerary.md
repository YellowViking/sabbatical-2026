## 2. The Schengen constraint вҡ пёҸ *(now the binding limit)*

Schengen = **90 days in any rolling 180-day period.** **Every stop above is Schengen** (Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Baltics, Slovenia, Croatia, Italy). Trimming Italy to ~20n lands the fall at **~83 nights вҖ” a healthy ~6вҖ“7-day buffer** under the cap (a delayed flight won't tip you into an overstay).

- **Balkans = the relief valve, deferred:** the western Balkans (Bosnia, Montenegro, Albania, N. Macedonia, Kosovo) are **non-Schengen вҖ” they don't burn your 90 days.** Saving them for a future trip keeps this fall clean; they could also be spliced in *another time* to pause the clock.
- **No mid-trip reset within this trip:** the whole thing spans <180 days, so a short non-Schengen break wouldn't add Schengen days here вҖ” the 90 cap is absolute for *this* window.
- **The play:** вүӨ90 days Aug 22 вҶ’ ~mid-Nov, **fly home from Italy**; a spring return needs a **~mid-May** date for a full fresh 90 (Feb/Mar вүҲ 0вҖ“25 days) вҖ” see В§8.

*Non-Schengen belt (for reference): W. Balkans, Turkey/Georgia, UK/Ireland, Morocco.*

## 4. Fall hubs вҖ” what to do & why each length

### Copenhagen вҖ” 3n *(NEW В· entry point)*
Nyhavn, Tivoli, Rosenborg, Christiania, the food scene, **Louisiana** modern-art museum (coast); cycle everywhere. **Day-trip to MalmГ¶/Lund** (35 min across the Гҳresund bridge вҖ” Turning Torso + a charming university town). *Land here instead of Oslo вҖ” вүҲ same airfare, a whole extra capital.*

### Gothenburg вҖ” 2n *(NEW)*
Sweden's laid-back 2nd city: Haga (fika + wooden houses), fish market (FeskekГҙrka), the southern archipelago by tram + ferry. On the CPHвҶ’Oslo line, so ~free to include.

### Oslo вҖ” 3n
Opera roof, Akershus, National Museum (*The Scream*), Vigeland, Munch, GrГјnerlГёkka; BygdГёy museums by ferry (Fram, Kon-Tiki).

### Stavanger + Preikestolen вҖ” 3n *(NEW В· the iconic hike)* вӯҗ
Gamle Stavanger (old wooden town) + street art; **Preikestolen / Pulpit Rock** (8 km / 4вҖ“5 h, huge payoff over Lysefjord), optional Lysefjord cruise. *Late-Aug = last of the good hiking window.*

### Bergen + fjords вҖ” 6n
Bryggen, FlГёibanen up Mt FlГёyen (ridge hike to Ulriken), KODE, Fish Market; **DIY Norway-in-a-Nutshell** (Bergen Railway вҶ’ FlГҘm Railway вҶ’ NГҰrГёyfjord cruise вҶ’ Stegastein); Aurlandsdalen hike. *Pack rain; check the FlГҘm cruise schedule.*

### Gotland / Visby вҖ” 3n *(NEW В· side-trip)*
Ferry from NynГӨshamn to **Visby** вҖ” an intact medieval walled Hanseatic town (UNESCO); the island's dramatic *rauk* sea-stacks, cliffs, and cycling. *(Late-Aug is the tail of its season вҖ” verify ferry frequency.)*
